∂ε∂ωji=∂ε∂ej∂ej∂yj∂yj∂vj∂vj∂ωji=ej⋅(−1)⋅φj′(vj)⋅yi=−ejφj′(vj)yi\begin{aligned} \frac{\partial \varepsilon}{\partial \omega_{ji}} &= \frac{\partial \varepsilon}{\partial e_j}\frac{\partial e_j}{\partial y_j} \frac{\partial y_j}{\partial v_j} \frac{\partial v_j}{\partial \omega_{ji}}\\ &= e_j\cdot (-1) \cdot\varphi_{j}'(v_j)\cdot{y_i}\\ &= -e_j\varphi_{j}'(v_j){y_i} \end{aligned} ∂ωji​∂ε​​=∂ej​∂ε​∂yj​∂ej​​∂vj​∂yj​​∂ωji​∂vj​​=ej​⋅(−1)⋅φj′​(vj​)⋅yi​=−ej​φj′​(vj​)yi​​

Δωji=−η∂ε∂ωji=ηδjyi\begin{aligned} \Delta\omega_{ji}&=-\eta\frac{\partial \varepsilon}{\partial \omega_{ji}}\\ &=\eta\delta_{j}y_{i}\\ \end{aligned} Δωji​​=−η∂ωji​∂ε​=ηδj​yi​​

当神经元 jjj是输出节点时，可以直接得出局域梯度如下：

δj=ejφj′(tj)\delta_{j} = e_{j}\varphi_{j}'(t_{j}) δj​=ej​φj′​(tj​)
